## 1. Overview

**1.1** This document sets out the shipping requirements that apply to Cards submitted to MintVault for grading or related services. It forms part of the contract between you and MintVault and is referenced from clause 9 of the Submission Agreement.

**1.2** The shipping tier required is determined by the total Declared Value of the submission (inbound) or the total value of Cards being returned (outbound).

**1.3** Shipping tiers protect both you and MintVault:

- **Inbound:** the right minimum standard ensures your Cards arrive safely, with appropriate transit insurance from the carrier.
- **Outbound:** the right minimum cover ensures MintVault's liability to you during return shipping is appropriate to the value of the Cards.

**1.4** MintVault may update this document from time to time. The current version is always available at mintvaultuk.com/shipping-requirements. Material changes that affect existing submissions will not be applied retrospectively; the version in force at the time of your submission applies to that submission.

## 2. Inbound shipping (Customer → MintVault)

**2.1** You are responsible for arranging inbound shipping and bearing the risk of loss or damage in transit to MintVault until MintVault confirms receipt (Submission Agreement clause 9.1).

**2.2** Minimum required inbound shipping method. Based on the total Declared Value of your submission, the following minimum shipping methods are required:

| Total Declared Value | Required inbound method (or equivalent) | Carrier insurance cover |
|---|---|---|
| Up to £500 | Royal Mail Tracked 48 | Up to £150 (Royal Mail default) |
| £500 – £2,500 | Royal Mail Special Delivery Guaranteed by 1pm | Up to £2,500 |
| £2,500 – £10,000 | Royal Mail Special Delivery Guaranteed by 1pm with Extra Compensation | Up to £10,000 |
| Over £10,000 | Insured courier (DPD, ParcelForce 24 with declared value cover, or similar) plus prior email approval from MintVault at support@mintvaultuk.com | Per carrier declaration |

**2.3** Equivalent methods from other UK carriers with equivalent tracking and insurance cover are acceptable, provided the carrier product offers tracking, signature on delivery, and the minimum cover stated in the table above.

**2.4** Consequences of not following the minimum standard.

(a) Risk of loss or damage in transit remains entirely with the Customer up to the point MintVault confirms receipt.

(b) If you ship below the required minimum standard and a loss or damage occurs in transit, MintVault is not obligated to accept the submission or to treat the loss under any custody or claim provision.

(c) Where a Card arrives damaged and MintVault reasonably believes the damage occurred in transit due to inadequate shipping, MintVault may refuse to grade the Card, document the damage, and return the Card to the Customer at the Customer's cost, without further liability.

(d) Where a Card arrives and MintVault determines the Declared Value materially exceeds the tier the Customer shipped at, MintVault may pause the submission, request that the Customer confirm the accurate Declared Value, and apply auto-upcharge under clause 5.3 of the Submission Agreement. This does not retrospectively change the inbound transit risk position.

**2.5** Packaging requirements. Regardless of shipping tier, you must:

(a) use a card saver, top-loader, or equivalent rigid inner protection for each Card;

(b) use bubble wrap or equivalent padding around each Card;

(c) use a rigid outer box (padded envelopes are not sufficient for Cards of any declared value);

(d) seal the package with tape; and

(e) not include the Declared Value or any indication of high-value contents visible on the outside of the package.

**2.6** Evidence. Keep the following until MintVault has confirmed receipt and grading has progressed:

(a) proof of postage / drop-off receipt with tracking number;

(b) photograph of the packaged parcel before sealing (showing Card(s) in packaging);

(c) photograph of the sealed parcel with tracking label visible; and

(d) for high-value submissions (£2,500+), video of packing and sealing is recommended.

**2.7** International submissions. MintVault accepts international submissions at its discretion. International shipping is subject to customs, duties, and additional verification. Contact support@mintvaultuk.com before submitting internationally for specific guidance. Customs declarations must be accurate; MintVault is not responsible for shipments seized or delayed due to inaccurate customs declarations by the Customer.

> **SOLICITOR REVIEW FLAG — Section 2:** Please confirm that mandating minimum inbound shipping standards with cover-reduction consequences for non-compliance is enforceable against consumers under CRA 2015 s.62 (fairness) and that the packaging requirements in 2.5 are reasonable conditions.

## 3. Outbound shipping (MintVault → Customer)

**3.1** MintVault arranges return shipping. The shipping tier is determined by the total value of Cards being returned (which may differ from the original submission total if some Cards were refused, no-graded, or retained for investigation).

**3.2** Outbound shipping tiers.

| Total value of returned Cards | Return shipping option | Customer pays | Cover |
|---|---|---|---|
| Up to £100 | Royal Mail Tracked 48 | £10.99 | £100 |
| £100 – £500 | Royal Mail Tracked 24 Signed For | £12.99 | £500 |
| £500 – £2,500 | Royal Mail Special Delivery Guaranteed by 1pm | £19.99 | £2,500 |
| £2,500 – £7,500 | Royal Mail Special Delivery Guaranteed by 1pm with Extra Compensation | £34.99 | £7,500 |
| £7,500 – £10,000 | DPD Insured Courier (or Royal Mail SD with max Extra Compensation) | £49.99 | £10,000 |
| Over £10,000 | Bespoke — MintVault quotes at submission or at point of return | Quoted | Per quote |

**3.3** Auto-selection. The Site selects the minimum required shipping tier automatically based on the Declared Value (or, where applicable, the total value of returned Cards). You may upgrade to a higher tier but you cannot downgrade below the required minimum.

**3.4** Payment. Outbound shipping cost is added to your submission fee at the point of submission. Where the value of returned Cards changes during grading (for example, due to no-grades reducing the total value), MintVault may refund the difference to the shipping component of the fee.

**3.5** Cover and MintVault's liability during return transit.

(a) MintVault arranges return shipping at the level you paid for. Up to the cover amount stated, MintVault is responsible for loss or damage in transit in line with the Submission Agreement clause 9.3.

(b) Above the cover amount, risk is with the Customer unless additional cover has been separately purchased through MintVault in writing at the time of submission.

(c) Claims for loss or damage in return shipping are raised under clause 14 of the Guarantee & Correction Policy within the 14-day inspection window (clause 10 of the Submission Agreement).

**3.6** Delivery arrangements.

(a) Return shipments require a signature on delivery where the shipping tier includes this.

(b) If you are not available at the address on the first delivery attempt, the carrier's standard redelivery and collection procedures apply.

(c) Where a parcel is returned to MintVault because the Customer failed to take delivery after reasonable attempts by the carrier, MintVault may hold the Cards until the Customer arranges redelivery and may charge a reasonable fee for a second shipment.

(d) Delivery address changes after dispatch are not always possible. Contact support@mintvaultuk.com as soon as possible if your address changes after submission.

**3.7** Outbound international shipping. International return shipping is at MintVault's discretion, subject to prior agreement and applicable carrier, customs, and sanctions restrictions. Pricing is bespoke.

> **SOLICITOR REVIEW FLAG — Section 3:** Please confirm the pass-through cost structure (Customer pays a fixed amount, MintVault arranges shipping) is transparent under CRA 2015 s.64 and does not create an unfair term under s.62.

## 4. Bespoke arrangements for Cards over £10,000

**4.1** Where the Declared Value of a single Card or the total Declared Value of a submission exceeds £10,000, bespoke shipping arrangements are required for both inbound and outbound. Please contact support@mintvaultuk.com before submitting.

**4.2** Bespoke arrangements may include:

(a) a specific carrier and service level agreed in writing;

(b) a declared-value insurance declaration;

(c) additional packaging requirements;

(d) identity verification under Submission Agreement clause 4A;

(e) a separately agreed Value Protection cover level above the Maximum Standard Cover; and

(f) a bespoke shipping cost.

**4.3** Bespoke submissions are not processed until a written bespoke agreement is in place. Submissions above £10,000 received without a bespoke agreement may be refused, returned at the Customer's cost, or held pending agreement.

## 5. Lost, delayed, or damaged shipments

**5.1** Inbound (before MintVault receives). Risk is with the Customer until MintVault confirms receipt. Contact your carrier to raise a claim. MintVault can provide supporting documentation (expected arrival date, confirmation of non-receipt) on request.

**5.2** Outbound (during return shipping). If a return shipment is lost, delayed, or damaged, contact support@mintvaultuk.com as soon as possible, and in any event within 14 days of the expected delivery date. We will:

(a) investigate with the carrier;

(b) provide tracking updates;

(c) if the shipment is confirmed lost or damaged, handle the claim under clause 14 of the Guarantee & Correction Policy, up to the cover level of the shipping tier paid for.

## 6. Packaging materials provided by MintVault

**6.1** MintVault returns Cards in appropriate protective packaging, including (as applicable) a rigid outer box, bubble wrap, Card savers or top-loaders, and fragile-item marking.

**6.2** MintVault does not provide pre-paid inbound shipping labels as standard. In some cases (for example, high-value bespoke submissions), MintVault may offer pre-paid labels at the Customer's cost.

## 7. Updates to this document

**7.1** MintVault may update these Shipping Requirements from time to time, including adjusting tier thresholds, cover amounts, pricing, and carrier options, in response to carrier changes, market conditions, or operational needs.

**7.2** Updates apply to new submissions from the date they take effect. Submissions already in progress are governed by the version in force at the time of submission.

**7.3** Material changes (for example, increases in Customer-facing pricing or reductions in cover levels) will be notified to registered users by email at least 30 days before taking effect.
